We provide setting up appointments and the B2B lead generation services. We create qualified sales leads, with named decision-makers or influencers – as defined by you – in the target markets and companies that you want to sell into, anywhere in the world. We have established Personalized Email marketing framework, and build our target databases specific to your brief, from the ground-up. You tell us the job function you need to speak with, the vertical sector, organization size and geography, and we research and build the dataset. Alongside this, we work with you on a compelling proposition for your services or solutions that is likely to capture the attention of your targets. Once we’ve done this, we systematically connect with the target market via email, and arrange qualified online meetings for your salespeople directly with the decision-makers that they want to talk with.
